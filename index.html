About The Game..

The player has found himself deep inside of a cave. In his predicament the player has come upon an idol that gives him the ability to move earth. After finding his way out of the cave using his newfound power, the idol leads the player to new locations with more idols that give allow him to Fire, water, and air. He must use his new found abilities to escape each location and continue finding these mysterious Artifacts.

The player's goal is to complete the level as fast as possible with the least possible flaws, the goal is to get the lowest time possible, time correlates to score.

Your Mission..

The player's goal is to complete all the levels in the game and eventually gain control of all abilities. For each level without running into or being interrupted by obstacles. the goal is to get the lowest time possible, time correlates to score.
